About GVCS Gulf

Gulf Valve Castings Specialities FZE is a Steel Foundry located in Hamriyah Free Zone Phase 2, Sharjah, United Arab Emirates. GVCS is well equipped with latest technologies, advanced automated machineries from leading foundry machinery manufacturer around the globe. Our Foundry is capable of producing Steel Castings for Valves, Pumps, Mining and Engineering Industry in Carbon steel, Alloy steel, Stainless Steel, Duplex and Special Steel as per customer request up to 4 ton single piece weight. Automatic moulding line can produce upto 10 moulds per hour with maximum size of 1800x1400x600/600 cake mould. In GVCS every cast we produce is a specially engineered art to meet the customers technical and quality requirements.
